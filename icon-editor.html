Instructions:

  1. Upload a PNG image using the button above
  2. Click and drag on the image to create a square selection
  3. Click "Crop Image" to crop to the selected area
  4. Click "Add Rectangle" to add a colored rectangle shape behind the image
  5. Drag the rectangle to reposition it, or drag the corner handle to resize
  6. Click "Save Image" to download the edited image with rectangle layer
